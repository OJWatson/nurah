# nurah: Testing causal models of mortality in crisis-affected populations

nurah is an R package designed as a research compendium, focused on
evaluating the feasibility of estimating mortality in humanitarian
crises. It utilises statistical models, directed acyclic graphs (DAGs),
and explores data availability challenges to produce robust and
actionable mortality estimates.
